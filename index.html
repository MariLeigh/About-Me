My name is Marissa Iannarone

As an associate with Forum Solutions LLC, I work with Seattle businesses and non-profits to build effective strategies for transformative growth and sustainable results. I'm proud to be a part of this growing women-owned firm.

Marissa has 5+ years of product and project management experience in the healthcare, global health, and federal sectors. From conducting healthcare infrastructure assessments to leading technology product development efforts, Marissa draws from nearly 4 years of experience living and working abroad to always bring a strategic perspective to client needs. Marissa's inquisitive nature, zest for taking on new challenges, and passion for authentic relationships ensures that the client is at the center of all that she does.

Marissa completed a Master’s degree in International Healthcare Management, Economics, and Policy at SDA Bocconi in Milan, Italy. She holds a Bachelor of Science in Public Health from the University of Washington. She is also a Pragmatic Marketing product management practitioner and Certified ScrumMaster.

In her free time Marissa is planning her next travel adventure, keeping up with her group-fitness instructor of a partner, and enjoying Seattle’s culinary culture.
